Who I Am

About Creviz Studio

A passionate 3D artist obsessed with detail, lighting, and storytelling through vertices, polygons and pixels.

Sculpting Stories in Three Dimensions.

I'm a freelance 3D artist with over 3 years of experience specialising in 3D environments and character design. From dense alien jungles to battle-worn warriors, I build worlds and beings that feel real, lived-in, and alive.

My pipeline revolves around Blender for modelling and rendering, ZBrush for high-poly sculpting, and Substance Painter for PBR texturing — giving every asset film-quality finish at game-engine-ready poly counts.

Whether you need a cinematic hero character, a sprawling fantasy landscape, or stylised props for your indie game, I deliver polished, production-ready assets on time.
